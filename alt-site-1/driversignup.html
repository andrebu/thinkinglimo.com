FAQ

How many trips must I do with Drivr?

You can do 1 or +50 trips a week, that's your choice. Working with us is completely flexible and you can keep your existing clients.

What car should I have?

Mercedes E and S Class or similar vehicle in a good condition and in the same category. If you're not sure if your vehicle meets the requirements, sign up anyway and our team will contact you. The car must be clear of stickers and kept clean at all times.

What paper work is required?

You need to upload all appropriate licences.

How do customers pay?

Via their credit card which we have on file. All charges, fraud etc. is handled by us.

Rates, commission and pay out

- The rates corresponds to the local markets and can be found our rates page.

- Commission is 80% to you on all trips and we pay directly to your bank account every week.

What iPhone is required?

If you have an iPhone 4 or newer you can run the Drivr app

How do I get the Drivr app?

When you are approved by our Operations team we will send you the app via email.

How do I know when there is work?

If you are offline and we have a lot of work, we will send you a text message.
