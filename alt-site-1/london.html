About DRIVR London

DRIVR London is a new technology driven ground transportation service in central London. We want to bring quality and great customer service back into the taxi business and we do so by combining modern technology with the best, and most service minded chauffeurs in the business.

Unlike our competitors like Addison Lee, we have developed our own booking and dispatching system, which allow us to run a more efficient fleet with lower fares and faster pick up times. In addition to being a more efficient system, our new booking platform has several advantages compared to other radio dispatching systems. On our booking apps for Android and iOS, customers get all the features they expect from a modern taxi booking app. Before you book, you get an estimated ETA and a price estimate, in addition to an overview of the rates. After a car is requested, the customer get a booking confirmation, details about the driver and can see the car approaching the pick up address live on the map. These features has normally only been available on native apps, but DRIVR London brings all of these advantages to customers who book their car online or by phone.

After booking a private hire car online or via our call centre the customers receives a SMS with a link so they can track their dispatch vehicle live on a map and get information about the driver, rates and live ETA’s. At arrival will the customer also receive an arrival confirmation on SMS so they don’t need to go outside, before the car arrives.

Reasons to choose DRIVR London:

  • Only professional and friendly licenced drivers
  • We calculate pick up times based on live traffic data
  • All jobs confirmed via SMS or notifications.
  • High quality cars, to taxi prices.

Welcome to London

Famous for it fantastic range of theatres, tourist attractions, shopping districts and football clubs, the majestic city of London needs little introduction. This vibrant city, has something to offer for everyone, no matter if it's your first visit, or if you are a borne and raised Londoner.

There are some historic must sees. We recommend a tour around Westminster to see Big Ben, get a birds view of the city from London Eye and visit to the Tower of London to see the Crown Jewels of the United Kingdom. In addition to the mandatory tourist sites, make sure to visit fashionable Notting Hill and the vibrant Portebello Market, pay a visit to the colourful Camden Market, shop till you drop in the famous Oxford and Regent Street and see a musical in West End entertainment district

If you are looking for things to do in London, we recommend yplan, a great app with a vide selection of events that even locals haven’t heard about. And don’t worry about the transportation, with the DRIVR app you can get picked up within 15 minutes and get a quality ride to your next destination. It could also be a good idea to get a oyster card, so you easily can jump on and off the London tube, which can be an experience by itself
